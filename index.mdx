## Welcome to Targeter

Targeter is a smart marketing platform that helps you create targeted marketing campaigns.

## Quick Start

Integrate with the Targeter API as a trusted third-party partner:

1. **Get Credentials** - Receive your issued API key and secret from Targeter
2. **Authenticate** - Exchange your credentials for JWT tokens
3. **Create Campaigns** - Programmatically assist in campaign creation for your clients
